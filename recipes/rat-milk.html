Rat Milk Formula Recipe

Return to Index

As a single transgender mother of 5, there is nothing I love more than finding natural and healthy ways to emulate breast milk, and there are few better alternatives than rats milk.

Allow your children to develop to their fullest potential with this nutrient-rich breast milk alternative!
We recommend weening off at approximately 200 months of age

Ingredients

  • 1 rat (pregnant)
  • Patience
  • 1 milk pump

Steps

  1. Acquire or source a pregnant rat from your local pet store.
  2. Bind the rats' legs to a surface of your choosing to prevent skittering.(We recommend two people for this step)
  3. Use your milk pump to milk the rat incessently until death, then repeat processes 1-3 until desired amount is yielded.
  4. Microwave and serve hot!
  5. Enjoy!

Rancid Meat Tartare Recipe

Return to Index

Unlike some acquired tastes, this dish is appealing and easy to implement for those with even the most unrefined palette.

The benefits of purging the gut microbiome are insurmountable and immediate.
Please refrain from consuming if you weren't alive during the cold war

Ingredients

  • ambiguous meat of your choosing
  • an open mind
  • one quail egg

Steps

  1. Acquire or source your meat of choice and promptly remove packaging
  2. Allow meat to age on a clean surface for approximately 1-2 years, exposing to moisture when necessary
  3. After meat is properly fermented (use the "smell-test"), use your hands to form a baseball of rancid meat
  4. Crack one fresh quail egg over top of a small indentation in the meat, and serve to houseguests
  5. Enjoy!

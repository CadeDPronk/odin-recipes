Perpetual knuckle-stew

Return to Index

Knuckle-stew is a twist on the cult-classic perpetual stew, savory, sweet, and always a surprise!

This low-maintenance dish is convenient, cost-effective, and a go-to choice for most late nights.
With only a few ingredients to get started, this dish cannot be easier to prepare...

Ingredients

  • squirrel knuckle
  • bone broth
  • tallow
  • tendons
  • refrigerator leftovers

Steps

  1. Start by turning your stovetop burner to the maximum heat setting (11 in Europe)
  2. Add tendons, squirrel knuckles, bone broth, and tallow simultaneously as your stew base starts to boil
  3. Gradually implement refrigerator leftovers, crumbs from around the house, and coins for taste
  4. Salt to taste, stirring once every minute or so for the first six months, then turn heat setting down to a low simmer for the rest of eternity
  5. Continually add knuckles as desired to maintain the perpetual stew, and enjoy as any meal of the day!
